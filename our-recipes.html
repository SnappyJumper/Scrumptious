As our database of recipes grows we've put together this sample menu to show you what you can do with our recipes! This menu is perfect for a fun night in with your friends or that family get-together you've been thinking of hosting.

Starter

Roasted Butternut Soup

Indredients:

  • 4 Large Butternuts
  • 3 Large Carrots
  • 4 Sticks of Celery
  • 1 Large Spanish Onion
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • Approx 1L of Vegetable Stock
  • A Good Glug of Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Cooking Info:

Prep time: 10mins

Oven Temp: 200C

Cooking time: 40mins

Servings: 5

Method:

  1. Preheat oven to 200C
  2. Take your butternuts and cut them vertically in half, scoop out and dispose of the seeds and leave the skins on.
  3. Arrange your prepped butternuts on a baking tray skin side down and drizzle with some of your oil and salt and pepper. Place in the oven to roast for about 30mins or until the flesh begins to brown.
  4. While your butternuts are roasting, take your carrots onions and celery and wash and peel them where applicable. Then chop them into large chunks. Crush your garlic and set aside separate from the other veg.
  5. Next in a saucepan heat up the rest of your oil at a nice low-medium heat. Once the pan is hot enough add your chopped veg and sweat(fry on low heat with no browning).Continue until the onion is translucent and then add your garlic. Keep to a low heat stirring regularly to be sure you don't burn the garlic. Take off the heat after 5 mins.
  6. Once your butternuts are cooked carefully take them off the tray and remove the flesh with a spoon leaving behind the skin. Place the flesh into your saucepan, return to the heat and add your stock. Be sure that your stock only just covers your vegetables, if it needs more just top op with water. Boil and reduce to a simmer until all the veg can be easily pierced with a fork then remove from the heat and blitz.
  7. Season to taste and serve
  8. Scrumptious!

Main

Chicken & Chorizo Jambalaya

Indredients:

  • 4 Breasts of Chicken
  • 250g Diced Chorizo
  • 2 Large Carrots
  • 1 Large Spanish Onion
  • 2 Sticks of Celery
  • 2 Red Peppers
  • 4 Garlic Cloves, Crushed
  • 2 Teaspoons of Smoked Paprika
  • 1 Tin of Chopped tomatoes
  • Approx 1L of Vegetable Stock
  • 350g of Long Grain Rice
  • A Good Glug of Olive Oil
  • A pinch of Cayenne Pepper to taste!
  • Salt and Pepper
  • A Few Sprigs of fresh Coriander and Parsley

Cooking Info:

Prep time: 10mins

Cooking time: Approx 1hr

Servings: 5

Method:

  1. Wash and peel all veg where applicable.
  2. Finely dice the carrot, onion, celery, peppers and set aside with the peppers separate.
  3. Cut the chicken into nice 2cm chunks and place it, along with the diced chorizo, into a saucepan with oil on a medium heat. Fry for about 3 mins.
  4. Add the veg, bar the peppers and garlic, and sweat (fry on medium heat with no colour) until the onion goes translucent. Then add your peppers and continue to sweat for 5 mins.
  5. Next add your garlic and paprika. Continue to sweat for another 5 mins being careful not to burn the garlic.
  6. Now add your stock, chopped tomatoes and rice, bring to a boil and simmer for about 12mins until the rice is cooked.
  7. Season to taste with salt pepper and Cayenne and leave to stand for a bit with the lid on.
  8. Now plate up and serve with a few sprigs of your fresh herbs!
  9. Scrumptious!
